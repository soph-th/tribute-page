A Tribute to Snails

So much better than slugs (where are their stylish hats?)

I love snails they are absolutely the best and you should love them too.

Reasons Snails Deserve Love

  1. They can be so tiny.
  2. They can be massive.
  3. They can probably travel up to 100kmh but choose not to.
  4. Gravity means nothing to them.
  5. All words mean nothing to them. They don't have ears.
  6. They have little boppy eyes on stalks.
  7. Mersnails exist.
  8. They can feel vibrations, especially good ones.
  9. They inspired this hit Beach Boys song.
  10. They leave a trail of destruction and/or slime.
  11. They have adorable kitten faces.
  12. They feast on the flesh of cuttlefish, much like their ancestors feasted on the flesh of the kraken.
